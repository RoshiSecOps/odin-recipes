Pizza

Description

This pizza dish is extremely easy to prepare and will leave you mouthwatering! It has been a staple in our diet for years(although we actually eat out and never make it ourselves)

Ingredient

  • A wallet
  • Cash in the wallet
  • Alternatively a debit/credit card
  • A pizza place nearby
  • The motivation to get up from the couch to eat

Steps

  1. Get up
  2. Get dressed
  3. Put shoes on
  4. Walk to the pizza joint
  5. Order what you want
  6. Pay
  7. Wait for order
  8. Finally, eat!
